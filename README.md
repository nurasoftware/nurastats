# NuraStats Analytics

**[NuraStats Analytics](https://nurastats.com) is an open source, privacy-friendly and lightweight web analytics alternative to Google Analytics.**

## A better alternative for Google Analytics
Google Analytics is difficult to understand and use, slow to load and privacy-invasive. It takes time to understand and requires training. We built NuraStats Analytics, a simple but powerful alternative.

## Data ownership of your web visitors
By using NuraStats Analytics, you protect the privacy of your visitors and keep ownership of your website data. You can self-host NuraStats Analytics on your own server. 

If you are using our hosted service (cloud), your data remain completely in control of your website and you own all of your data too. 

NuraStats Analytics is build using Laravel Framework.
